### Project Overview
---
The purpose of this data analysis project is to track the rate of attrition, that is, the figure showcasing how many employees left the incubator hub workforce over a period of time based on their departments, education fields and gender. This analysis is carried out by the Human Resource department in order to focus on how to improve on employee relationship and to boost their performances.
